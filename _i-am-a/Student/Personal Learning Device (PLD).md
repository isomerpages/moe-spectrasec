<h4><strong>What does the PLD mean to... </strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Schools - </strong>A refined curriculum that is focused on learning
through blended learning pedagogy.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Teachers -</strong> To bring the art of teaching to the next level,
i.e. blended learning through an active learning process.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Students -</strong> Each student will have a learning device to
enable them to be equipped with essential digital skills for self-directed
and collaborative learning.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Parents -</strong> Each child will be enabled to learn essential
digital skills of Finding, Thinking, Applying and Creating new knowledge
and skills, for a meaningful life.</p>
</li>
</ul>
