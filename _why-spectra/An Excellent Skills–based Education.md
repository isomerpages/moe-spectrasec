<h5><strong>A Seamless Vocational Education Pathway</strong></h5>
<p>Spectra offers a dual-certification programme: our students will complete
and graduate with a&nbsp;<strong>Singapore-Cambridge Secondary Education Certificate (SEC)</strong>&nbsp;and
an&nbsp;<strong>ITE Skills Subject Certificate (ISSC)</strong>. The ITE
Skills Subject provides hands-on vocational education in an authentic learning
environment for our students.</p>

<h5><strong>Unique Vocational Education CCAs</strong></h5>
<p>Spectra also offer opportunities such as learning journeys to ITE campuses,
workshops by industry partners, preparation for Early Admission Exercise
(EAE) to ITE, and unique CCAs to equip our students for their ITE course
of choice.</p>
<p>Through a diverse range of unique CCAs, we empower students to aspire
toward their dreams. Our students discover new interests, develop their
talents and build on their strengths. Our dedicated teachers encourage
students to experiment, learn new skills and pursue their passion in the
following CCAs:&nbsp;<strong>Young Chefs, Young Creators, Young Engineers, and Urban Farmers.</strong>
</p>
